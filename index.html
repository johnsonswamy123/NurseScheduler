How to Use

  1. Open Nurse Master page and add nurses with roles (Staff Nurse, Incharge, Supervisor, Superintendent).
  2. Login using the username/password of the nurse who is working on the duty roster.
  3. Use Duty Assignment to assign shifts.
  4. Use Calendar for monthly overview, and Duty Roster for the full list.
  5. Staff Nurses raise requests on the Requests & Approvals page; higher roles approve according to hierarchy.
